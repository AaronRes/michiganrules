Greatest Wolverines Football Stretch in a Century

After going through hard times, with occasional high points, the legendary James Joseph Harbaugh led his Wolverines to a National Championship and the first three of four victories over ohio. These are my favorite moments from the past 4 years.

  1. Aidan Hutchinson and David Ojabo Wrecking ohio in 2021 - starting it all






  2. Donovan Edwards taking one of his two 70 yarders to the house...



  3. Blake Corum takes it to the house following Zak Zinter's terrible injury on an inspired run



  4. Rob Moore intercepts ohio, finishing off the 2023 game



  5. Kalel Mullings converts a critical first down and more to seal the 2024 victory over the future national champs, whose seniors never beat Michigan. Quite a shame.



Now we await with baited breath the Sherrone Moore era, having bested ohio and alabama again, along with landing a young rockslinger in David Underwood. The future is bright in Ann Arbor. Go Blue!
